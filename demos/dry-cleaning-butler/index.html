About Dry Cleaning Butler

At Dry Cleaning Butler, we're a family-owned operation dedicated to providing exceptional dry cleaning services to the Eagle, Idaho community and beyond. With over 40 years of industry experience, our owner, Mark, ensures every garment receives meticulous care.

We pride ourselves on quick service, superior stain removal, and an eco-friendly cleaning process using organic solvents. We believe in going the extra mile, offering free button replacements and professional alterations to ensure your clothes always look their best.
