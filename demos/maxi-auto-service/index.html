About Maxi Auto Service

Since 1980, Maxi Auto Service has been the trusted name in auto repair for the Chattanooga community. We are committed to providing top-quality, honest service for all makes and models, ensuring your vehicle is safe and reliable on the road.

Our experienced technicians use the latest diagnostic tools to accurately identify and fix issues, from routine maintenance to complex engine repairs. We believe in transparent pricing and clear communication, so you always know what to expect.
