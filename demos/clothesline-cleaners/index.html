About Clothesline Cleaners

For over 40 years, Clothesline Cleaners has been a staple in the Boise and Meridian communities, providing top-notch dry cleaning and laundry services. We are committed to using state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly practices to ensure your clothes receive the best care.
