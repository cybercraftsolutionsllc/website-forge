About C & R Roofing

With over 45 years of experience, C & R Roofing is a veteran-owned business dedicated to serving Chattanooga, Ooltewah, and Hixson, TN. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and honest service has built a reputation you can trust.

We specialize in comprehensive roofing solutions, from new installations and replacements to meticulous repairs and maintenance. We stand by our work, ensuring durability and aesthetic appeal for your home or business.
